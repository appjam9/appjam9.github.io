Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 june 2026

Welcome to Lingoflash ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use Lingoflash:

  • User-Generated Content: To generate flashcards using AI, you may input text or prompts. This data is processed to provide the service.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a subscription or features via Google Play In-App Billing, your payment details are processed securely by Google. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Device & Advertising Data: We collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data to show relevant ads and analyze app performance.

2. AI Flashcard Generation

How we use AI: Lingoflash utilizes Artificial Intelligence to generate flashcard content based on your inputs. When you use this feature, your input text is sent to our AI processing partner to generate the cards. We do not sell your specific input text to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Lingoflash uses the following third-party services, which may collect data as described below:

Google AdMob & Analytics:
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization").
Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Billing:
For processing In-App Purchases (IAP), we use Google Play Billing. Transaction data is handled by Google according to their privacy standards. We only receive a confirmation of purchase and the item purchased.
Google Payments Privacy Policy

AI API Providers:
To generate flashcards, your prompts may be sent to third-party AI API providers (such as OpenAI or similar). These providers process your request solely to return the generated content.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement standard industry security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Your flashcard decks may be stored locally on your device or synced to a cloud server (if applicable) to allow access across multiple devices.

5. Children's Privacy

Lingoflash is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
