PART 1: PRIVACY POLICY

We believe your mornings are personal. This section explains what data we collect to wake you up effectively and how we fiercely protect your privacy.

1. Data Collected

To provide a functional and gamified alarm experience, Rousr processes specific operational data:

  • Wake-up Logs & History: Times you successfully woke up, skipped missions, or snoozed alarms.
  • Alarm Settings: Your scheduled times, chosen ringtones, mission difficulty, and specific alarm configurations.
  • Subscription Status: Identifying if you hold an active Premium subscription tier.

2. Data Storage and Sharing

Local Storage Only: All your personal alarm data, settings, and wake-up logs are securely stored locally on your device via an encrypted Room database. We do not transmit, host, or back up your wake-up habits to external servers.

No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except for the explicit technical requirements of managing ads or premium billing (detailed below).

3. Required System Permissions

Rousr relies on core device permissions to execute its primary features, specifically the interactive wake-up missions:

Core Functionality

Alarms & Schedule

Required to set precise alarm triggers, overlay the wake-up screen on top of other apps, and prevent device sleep during an active alarm.

Core Functionality

Notifications

Required to show upcoming alarm reminders, missed alarms, and persistent status icons indicating an alarm is scheduled.

Interactive Missions

Camera Access

Strictly used for "Photo Missions" (e.g., scan a barcode or take a picture of a specific object to dismiss the alarm). Photos are processed locally and discarded immediately.

Interactive Missions

Microphone Access

Strictly used for "Voice Missions" (e.g., speaking a specific phrase to wake up). Audio data is analyzed locally and never recorded or transmitted.

Privacy Guarantee: Camera and Microphone data is processed in real-time within your device's memory. We do not save, record, or upload your photos or voice snippets anywhere.

4. Third-Party Services

To keep the app running, we integrate with trusted Google services:

  • Google Play Billing: Handles secure transactions if you choose to subscribe to premium features. We never see your credit card data.
  • Google AdMob: Serves advertisements to basic tier users. AdMob may utilize hardware identifiers to display contextually relevant ads.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

Because Rousr operates entirely on a local Room database, you maintain absolute control over your information. To request data deletion, you simply need to:

  • Clear the app's storage data via your device's Settings menu, OR
  • Uninstall the application from your device entirely. This permanently purges all logs and settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Rousr is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal or usage data from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that a child has provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us immediately.
